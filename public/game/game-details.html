About the game

Spider-Man: Miles Morales is an action-adventure game where you play as Miles Morales, defending New York City. In Peter Parker's absence, Miles must become his own Spider-Man. Defeat enemies with unique powers like Venom Blast and Camouflage and keep the city safe.
